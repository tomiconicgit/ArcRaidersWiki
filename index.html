Pro Tips & Strategies

Survival Basics

Scan for ARC before engaging. Use cover, prioritize extraction. Farm hidden caches for blueprints.

Blueprint Farming

Focus on high-value areas like relay stations on Dam. Join Harvester events for rare drops.

Team Play

Coordinate flanks against ARC. Share resources and use lure grenades to distract enemies.

Extraction Tips

Use Raider Hatches for safe exits but balance with costs. Avoid high-traffic standard points.
